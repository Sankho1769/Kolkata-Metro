METRO REGULATIONS

For the safety and comfort of all passengers, the following items cannot be taken inside the metro premises.

  • Raw fish, meat
  • Sealed or unsealed charged batteries
  • Large quantities of fish/meat (for business)
  • Objects longer than 4 feet
  • Dynamite, explosives, flammable substances
  • Screwdrivers, hammers, knives, saws, tools
  • Petrol, acid, liquids, large batteries
  • Toxic substances, tear gas
  • Dead bodies, skeletons, human body parts
  • Carcasses of dead animals

If caught carrying these, a fine will be imposed.
